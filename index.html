01 / What we believe

Strategy without execution is just an opinion. Execution without strategy is just noise.

Principle i. The voice is yours.We don't invent your brand voice. You already have it, written down or simply lived. Our job is recognising it, sharpening it, and putting it on everything you ship, consistently, without diluting it along the way.

Principle ii. No fluff. Ever.We don't ship buzzwords in a beautiful template. We build things your team can use on Monday morning: a system, a plan, a calendar that fills itself. Anything less isn't finished, and we'll say so ourselves.

Principle iii. Named humans, not hidden.When we bring in specialists, you see who they are. Our network is credited, curated, and senior, and you meet them when the work starts. No mystery interns behind the deliverable, no markup hiding in the hours.

Principle iv. AI does the generic work.Every team we meet loses hours to work a machine should do: first drafts, repurposing, reporting. We set up the prompts and automations that take it over, so the humans can do the part that needs them. Our own practice has run this way since 2023.

02 / How we help

The work lives in content, brand voice, and marketing operations. Performance campaigns, SEO, and channel-specific execution come from the Network, briefed and led by us.

01

Marketing Audit

The starting point. Two to three weeks looking at where your marketing actually stands. What's working, what's leaking, what's missing, where to invest next. You leave with a prioritised list: typically 10 to 15 items, ranked by what they cost you. If the list isn't worth the fee, you don't pay it.

NOT READY? THE SCAN · ONE WEEK

You hand over the numbers, we add what we see from the outside, and you get a written conclusion with five recommendations. No interviews, that's the audit's job. Upgrade within two months and it comes off the audit fee.

THE WORKING SESSION · HALF A DAY

One problem, one room (or one call), four hours. You bring the mess, we leave with a decision and the first three steps written down. Counts toward the Audit if you upgrade within two months.

02

Kickstart

A working content system, set up in your tools, that your team can run without us. Three to four weeks of build. Prompts, templates, and one or two real automations doing the work week after week. Handover session and a short runbook.

03

Partner

ADVISORY

A fixed weekly slot. Strategy, sparring, and keeping the plan honest. Your team executes.

WORKING

07 / Questions

Who is this for?

Teams at growing companies that have their own marketing people. We work next to your team, not instead of it. Founders without a team yet usually start with the Audit.

Do you also execute?

Yes. Strategy and execution by the same hands is the whole point. When a project needs a specialist (design, copy, photo, development), we bring one in from the Network, credited by name.

What does it cost?

Every engagement is scoped to your situation, so the number lives in the proposal, not in a rate card. After our first 30 minutes you get a one-page proposal: what we'd do, in what order, and what it costs. No templates, no surprises.

Do we need to be in Amsterdam?

No. Based in Amsterdam, working globally. Most collaborations run remote with a kickoff in person where it helps.
